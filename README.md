#  Customer Segmentation Analysis

##  Problem Statement

The goal of this project is to identify different types of customers based on their annual income and spending behavior, helping businesses target the right audience.

---

##  Dataset

Mall Customer Segmentation Dataset (Kaggle)
Includes customer details such as age, income, and spending score.

---

##  Data Cleaning

* Checked for missing values
* Verified data types
* Ensured dataset consistency

---

##  Exploratory Data Analysis

* Visualized distribution of age and income
* Analyzed relationship between income and spending score
* Identified visible clusters in customer behavior

---

##  Manual Segmentation

Customers were grouped into categories:

* Premium Customers (High income, high spending)
* Careful Customers (High income, low spending)
* Impulsive Customers (Low income, high spending)
* Budget Customers (Low income, low spending)

---

##  Key Insights

* High-income customers who spend less represent untapped potential
* Premium customers are the most valuable segment
* Some customers spend heavily despite lower income
* Majority of customers fall into mid-level spending behavior

---

##  Tools Used

* Python (Pandas, Matplotlib)
